Who we are

Pararthya is the registered NGO working on the major issue of poverty, environment deterioration, habitat protection, skill development/training, child education and women’s empowerment with the skilled and dedicated professionals.

Because of the diversity of voices and perspectives in our team, we have managed to create thematic cluster around which act as hub of expertise on numerous issues like Sustainable Development, Environmental Protection and Poverty Alleviation Goals. With a focused approach, to deal with the complex multifaceted reality we are working towards evolving into being one of key players in the sector of sustainable development.

We are always striving towards transparency and inclusiveness for the welfare of our society.  Pararthya has twofold purpose of furthering society’s interests as we are focusing upon environment protection while improving standard of living of most marginalized section of society in the developing country. We are into inclusive growth and sustainable development by enhancing the quality of lives of people from the marginalized and vulnerable communities there by empowering them through innovative and sustainable solutions.

Our Values

Our philosophy is in the interest of earth and the benefit of mankind we cntribute to the creation of the society that practices sustainable development. PSDECS envisions a world where every citizen is capable of earning sustainable livelihood for his family. We envision us a national level resource agency in India through its direct work as well as through its partnerships and capacity building activities with like minded organizations and individuals.

Our mission is to brand/make India into the worldwide sourcing hub for skilled workforce by becoming a catalyst of change for transforming the life of most marginalized urban and rural stakeholders by making them self-dependent and curbing poverty. Pararthya is slowly spreading its wings and started focusing on long term sustainability options for these marginalized people through various livelihood projects and helping them to enhance their skills for better opportunities.

Take Actions

Sustainable development is development that meets the needs of the present without compromising the ability of future generations to meet their own needs. ( Brundtland Report, 1987 ) The resources of the Earth are finite and they are dwindling at an alarming rate, the quality of our natural environment is deteriorating.

The resource depletion and environmental degradation is threatening the physical security, economic wellbeing, and health of many people, but the poor and most disadvantaged and deprived communities are most vulnerable. So it’s very important to take initiatives to support these people and make them capable of handling this situation. Conserving the biodiversity and reducing poverty are vital for sustainable development. 

So ,for achieving sustainability on earth, conservation and development should go hand in hand. The environmental conditions of the Earth are getting worse day by day. Increasing population, mass migration to cities, increasing energy use and soaring carbon dioxide emissions mean humanity is putting a greater squeeze on the planet's resources than ever before.

Particularly hard hit is the diversity of animals and plants, upon which many natural resources such as clean water and air is based. So it’s very important to conserve this biodiversity and natural resources.

Our Projects

Save Okhla Bird Sanctuary - Pararthya is working closely with the conservation of Okhla Bird Sanctuary, a wetland heaven for over 300 bird species.

It is now one of the 466 IBAs (Important Bird Areas) in India. Lately, there has been an observant decrease in population of migratory birds due to increase in air, noise and water pollution in the Yamuna River, further shrinking the habitat of marshlands and water areas.

With an aim of reviving the sanctuary, Pararthya is carrying out some interventions with Uttar Pradesh Forest Department.
